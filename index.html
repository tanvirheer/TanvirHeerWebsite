About

I was a an undergraduate student at UCSC as well, majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering. My interests include power electronics, robotics, and sensor design.

Papers

I have not published any papers yet, but I have worked on a few projects. Below are some descriptions of the projects I worked on so far.

Smart Wingsail Project

Made a low power modular wingsail that can be used as a means of propulsion for any watercraft. It takes in the wind direction and the desired user speed as inputs and oreients itself at the optimal angle needed to achieve the thrust. This allows watercraft to reduce fuel consumption since now any boat can harness wind power without years of sailing experience.

SEADS Project

Designed a easy to install and non intrusive home energy monitoring device. It uses a high sampling ADC to measure the power consumption from the main power line, and desegregates the different energy signatures to determine what appliance is currently active.
